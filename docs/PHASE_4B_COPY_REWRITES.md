## Overview

This document proposes rewritten copy for all major sections of the AbiliLife landing page. Each rewrite is designed to:

- Be clear, simple, and grounded in pilot reality
- Avoid buzzwords, overpromising, and vague ecosystem language
- Distinguish "Mobility pilot" from the future super app vision
- Build trust through honest, specific communication

**DO NOT IMPLEMENT** until approved by founders.
